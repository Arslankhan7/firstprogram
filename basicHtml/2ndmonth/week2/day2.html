Rolls Royce Ghost

Manufacturer: Rolls-Royce Motor Cars, a British luxury automobile maker. Model Description: The Rolls-Royce Ghost is a luxury sedan that sits below the flagship Phantom in the Rolls-Royce lineup. It's known for its combination of exquisite craftsmanship, advanced technology, and a smooth, refined ride. Performance: The Ghost typically features a powerful engine, providing a smooth and quiet ride. Rolls-Royce emphasizes a "magic carpet ride" experience, focusing on comfort and luxury. Interior Features: Rolls-Royce vehicles, including the Ghost, are celebrated for their lavish interiors. The Ghost's cabin often includes premium materials, handcrafted wood veneers, leather upholstery, and customizable options for personalization. Technology: Rolls-Royce integrates cutting-edge technology into its vehicles. The Ghost may come equipped with advanced infotainment systems, high-quality audio, and a host of driver assistance features. Design: Rolls-Royce cars are known for their iconic design, characterized by a bold grille, the Spirit of Ecstasy ornament, and a sense of timeless elegance. The Ghost typically embodies a contemporary yet classic design. Price: The Rolls-Royce Ghost is positioned as a top-tier luxury vehicle, and its price reflects its exclusivity and craftsmanship
